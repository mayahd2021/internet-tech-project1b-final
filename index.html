About MEISA

Music & Entertainment Industry Student Association (or MEISA) is a student organization focused on developing the skills of Loyola University New Orleans students interested in working in the entertainment industry. Members benefit from opportunities to perform on campus, attend networking events, listen to guest speakers, and promote their work through the organization blog and website. MEISA aims to be a community that allows members to network with other members and offer their skills while benefiting from being able to access and learn from the skills of other members.
